Our Story

Growing companies get stuck between a bookkeeper who records the past and a $300K CFO they can't yet justify. We built Squad to close that gap.

We spent our careers in the seats that matter — as CFOs, operators, and investors inside the businesses we now serve. We sat in the diligence rooms, ran the models under pressure, and made the calls that moved enterprise value. And we watched too many good companies stall in the same place: too big for a bookkeeper, too early for a full C-suite.

The traditional options both fail you. A solo fractional consultant gives you one person's bandwidth and one person's blind spots. A big firm gives you a rotating cast who bill you to learn your business. We built a third way — a squad.

Every client gets a dedicated team that knows your business cold, backed by a deep bench of specialists — M&A counsel, tax CPAs, AI engineers, sector operators — who step in the moment complexity calls. You get continuity and depth, without hiring either. And because we've been AI-native since day one, that squad moves faster, catches more, and sees further than a team twice its size.

Three practices grew out of that model — Finance, Intelligence, and Family — mapped to the three questions every ambitious company or family eventually asks: How do we fund and steer this? How do we run and improve it? And how do we protect and grow the wealth it creates? One squad answers all three, in one conversation.

The name says it all. Squad — because we work as one team, always.
